Kisan Baburao "Anna" Hazare is an Indian soical activist who led movements to promote rural development, government transparency, as well as to expose and punish government corruption. Hazare has been involved in grassroots reformation causes since the early 1990s. An anti-corruption movement organized in Spring 2011 by Hazare that involved thousands of Indian citizens succeeeded when the government establihed the Lokpal Anti-Corruption Government Agency in December 2013.
